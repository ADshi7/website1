FAQ

Any new idea comes with many questions being asked. To get a better understanding, here are some of our more frequently asked questions. What is the point of this website if I am not able to purchase anything? As we have stated, this particular website is intended as a space where you can just look and take inspiration from. It’s intended as an online browsing space, as each and every item takes a focus on itself. We want our users to enjoy the experience of scrolling and checking back in weekly for new items and drops. We also want users to enjoy the community we have built and interact with one another. What if there is nothing I like on this website, and what if it is not my style of choice? We try to cater to as many different people as we can; if these items are things that you don’t like, then this particular website might not be suitable for you. Given that we take feedback very seriously, we will try our best to incorporate as much as we can to suit the needs of everyone. How will I know how the items look if it’s not represented and shown on anyone? As we have said, in the past, this website is strictly to give attention to the items itself instead of who is wearing it, and how it looks like on others. As we are not trying to sell these items, but instead show off the latest wanted items.
